    Café Paradiso represents a merger of two cultures – Italian and American – with love. Conceived, built and run by a mixture of 35 years of living in Italy in Pietrosante, an artist community outside of Florence, and being raised in the Bronx.

    Our Food

    Café Paradiso is open for breakfast, lunch and dinner. Enjoy our pastries and espresso for breakfast, our salads and sandwiches for lunch or our pizzas and pastas for dinner.
