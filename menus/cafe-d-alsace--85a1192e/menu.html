    • The Jewel of the Upper East Side

    A culinary cornerstone of Manhattan, Cafe D'Alsace has been a neighborhood favorite for nearly two decades. Awarded two stars by the New York Times, we offer Alsatian cuisine in a familiar, yet uniquely special setting, blending German and French culinary influences and service traditions. 

    Our Story

    Tradition & Excellence

    So named for the Alsace region of northeastern France, our diverse menu features classic Alsatian favorites, alongside ever-changing seasonal specials. Think Tarte Flambée, Choucroute Garnie and our exceptional house-made desserts, plus a beverage program that includes over 130 beers, and a dedicated in-house cicerone.
