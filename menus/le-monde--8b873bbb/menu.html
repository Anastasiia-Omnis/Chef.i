Our Food

Classic French cuisine meets creative cooking at Le Monde, where Executive Chef Segundo offers dishes from Escargot and Steak Tartare, to house-made crêpes and freshly baked flatbreads.

Recognized by Wine Spectator for our exceptional wine selection, our menus channel the Loire Valley in both style and service, inviting you to relax and raise a glass to shared moments in our welcoming brasserie.

Your Neighborhood Brasserie

Located in the heart of the Upper West Side, Le Monde has been serving the historic Morningside Heights community since 1998. Named after the French newspaper Le Monde (The World), our brasserie is truly wordly — a place where locals, students and visitors alike gather together to enjoy breakfast, lunch, dinner and drinks.
