About Harold

A legacy of New York deli excellence

The Legend Continues

Harold brings decades of experience from operating two of New York's most beloved dining institutions - the legendary Claremont Diner and the world-famous Carnegie Deli.

Now in Edison, New Jersey, Harold has carefully combined the very best elements from both establishments to create something truly special - a place where traditional New York deli culture lives on.

Every sandwich, every meal, every dish is prepared with the same attention to quality and generous portions that made both the Claremont and Carnegie Deli legendary.
