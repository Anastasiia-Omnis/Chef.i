Our Story

New Choy Hing Restaurant offers authentic and delicious tasting Chinese cuisine in Brooklyn, NY. New Choy Hing's convenient location and affordable prices make our restaurant a natural choice for dine-in or take-out meals in the Brooklyn community. Our restaurant is known for its variety of tastes and high quality fresh ingredients. Come and experience our friendly atmosphere and excellent service.
