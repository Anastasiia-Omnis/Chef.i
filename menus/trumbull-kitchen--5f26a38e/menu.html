Culinary Innovation for Over 20 Years

Max's Trumbull Kitchen is a stylish and eclectic restaurant located in the heart of downtown Hartford. Under the umbrella of Max Hospitality, we’ve been providing high-quality and innovative dining experiences since 2001.

At Max's Trumbull Kitchen, we take pride in offering a diverse menu that combines flavors from different cultures around the world. Our chefs use only the freshest ingredients to create inventive and unique dishes that emphasize intense flavors and satisfying textures.

What To Expect On Our Menus

Under the direction of Executive Chef and Partner, Chris Torla, Max's Trumbull Kitchen is known for its globally eclectic menu that features cross-cultural dishes and exciting twists and variations of familiar food from around the world.

Chris’s menu features a collection of tasters and tapas as well as larger plates and entrées. Items range from the TK version of macaroni & cheese to steamed mussels fra diavlo or a Chinese chicken salad. Each dish is exquisitely prepared to impress.
