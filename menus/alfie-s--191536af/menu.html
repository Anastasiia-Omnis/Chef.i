    • Welcome to Alfie's

    Stationed on the corner of West 53rd Street & 9th avenue- you will find Alfie's, a neighborhood restaurant & bar. Serving New York City’s Hell’s Kitchen neighborhood since 2012. 

    A place where you can come in for a great meal, & friendly hospitality. With enough hops to keep you happy, gourmet comfort food to feed your soul, & enough Bourbon, whiskey, & rye to keep you satisfied & coming back for more.

    Our kitchen is open for lunch, dinner and weekend bottomless brunch.

    We feature 20 local rotating craft beers on tap, seasonal cocktails and an exclusive old fashioned menu.

    Alfie’s is conveniently located in close proximity to the NYC Theatre District, Times Square, & Columbus Circle.

    Food and Beverage

    Offering elevated comfort food for lunch, dinner and brunch. We put our own twist on traditional dishes from around the world.

    At Alfie’s we are committed to working with local purveyors that provide the highest quality for our guests, the environment and community.

    We feature 20 local rotating craft beers on tap. Our seasonal cocktails and exclusive signature old fashioned menus are crafted with care and creativity.
