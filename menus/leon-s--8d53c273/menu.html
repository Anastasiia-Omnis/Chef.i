    Private Dining & Events

    Our intimate, lovingly-designed Private Dining Room (accommodates 22 seated guests, 30 standing) and features arched ceilings, walnut-lined walls, and an antique Italian patinated brass chandelier from the 1940s. The entire restaurant is also available for buyouts on a limited basis.

    Celebrate With Us!

    In the spirit of our sibling Anton’s in the West Village, Leon’s is a convivial gathering place for our surrounding community. We're open every day from early morning until late in the evening, serving continuously from breakfast through dinner. Leon’s spotlights our deep passion for Italian cuisine, alongside thoughtful interpretations of treasured family recipes and culinary traditions passed down by way of Egypt and France.
