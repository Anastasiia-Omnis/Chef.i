Market Table

welcome to market table

Market Table's farm to table menu celebrates the best of the market's bounty, influenced by Chef Mike Price's childhood on his family farm. Market Table welcomes all of its guests with genuine warmth and hospitality, and our seasonal menus are a staple for our neighborhood American restaurant. Located at the corner of Carmine and Bedford streets in the West Village, Market Table’s floor-to-ceiling windows and wonderful ambiance offer an unparalleled view onto village life, perfect for contemporary dining and private events.

Reservations

Market Table is open for indoor and outdoor dining. While reservations are recommended, we also set aside a few tables at each service for walk-in guests so come on by!

For reservation assistance, large parties, or just to say hi, please give us a call at (212) 255-2100 or e-mail reservations@markettablenyc.com.
