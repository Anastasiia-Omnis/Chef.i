FAQ

FAQ

How do I place an order for catering?

You can place an order for catering here.

Do you have a catering minimum?

We ask that you meet a $150 pick-up & delivery minimum.

Can I place an order for the same day?

All orders are made fresh at Daily Provisions, we kindly ask that you place your order 24 hours in advance. Extra-large orders may require additional notice.

What if I have to cancel my order?

Please note you will not be able to cancel your order yourself. Instead, please email us at catering@dailyprovisions.co and we will do our best to accommodate the cancellation. We charge a fee of 50% of your order total for cancellations less than 24 hours in advance of your scheduled pick-up/delivery time.

How do I make edits to my order?

Please note you will not be able to make edits to your order yourself. Instead, please email catering@dailyprovisions.co and we are happy to help! While we will do our best to accommodate all change requests, those made less than 24 hours in advance of your scheduled pick-up/delivery time are not guaranteed.

Does my order include plates, napkins, etc.?

Upon request, we offer plates, cups, napkins and/or cutlery free of charge.


PICK-UP, DELIVERY & SET UP

Can I order for delivery?

We offer delivery in Manhattan, Brooklyn, Long Island City, Jersey City/Hoboken and Boston/Cambridge and charge a flat catering delivery fee of $40 for orders up to $300 and $50 for orders $300+.

How does pick-up work for catering orders?

You can select your pick up time within restaurant operating hours and we will have the order ready for you at the Daily Provisions location of your choice. Pick-up times vary per location.

When should I request delivery drop-off?

When choosing a delivery time, we recommend requesting an earlier time to allow for any delays due to traffic, building check-in procedures, etc.

What if my address is outside your delivery zone?

While we can’t guarantee delivery to out-of-zone locations, feel free to reach out to catering@dailyprovisions.co and we will do our best to accommodate.

Will your team help set up my order? 

We offer drop-off catering only and are not be able to guarantee help with set up.

ALLERGEN INFORMATION

Allergen information is listed under each menu item on our catering order page. Please note that all items are produced in the same kitchen so there is always a risk of cross contamination.

PAYMENT

We are a tax-exempt organization, do you offer tax exemption?

Yes! We offer tax exemption on catering orders for organizations with tax-exempt status. When placing your order online, please check the tax-exempt box on the checkout page and enter your tax-exempt certificate number. Once your order has been placed, please email a copy of your tax-exempt certificate to catering@dailyprovisions.co. Please note that if you have already placed an order with tax included, we are not able to remove the tax and issue a refund.

Can I pay for my order with a Daily Provisions or USHG gift card?

Yes! Please reach out to us at catering@dailyprovisions.co and we will help place the order for you.

Still have questions? Please reach out to us at catering@dailyprovisions.co
