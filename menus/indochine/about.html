About

Food is more than just nourishment—it’s a connection to culture and community.

Our menu features a blend of traditional Vietnamese dishes and creative fusion items that highlight the diversity of flavors our cuisine has to offer. From steaming bowls of phở, bursting with aromatic herbs and savory broths, to crisp and flavorful banh mi sandwiches, each dish is prepared with the finest ingredients to ensure an authentic experience. Our popular vermicelli noodle bowls are perfect for a hearty, yet refreshing meal, while our signature Vietnamese street tacos bring an innovative twist to classic street food.

Our menu is designed to cater to all tastes and preferences.

Whether you’re looking for a light, healthy lunch or a satisfying dinner, our menu is designed to cater to all tastes and preferences. We also offer a range of vegan and gluten-free options.

Over the years, we’ve built a strong connection with the Rutgers University community, locals, students, and visitors who make New Brunswick such a unique place. To our longtime regulars, thank you for your support, because of you we’ve been able to thrive for more than a decade. And to those visiting us for the first time, we welcome you into the Indochine family.

We look forward to serving you for many more years to come!
