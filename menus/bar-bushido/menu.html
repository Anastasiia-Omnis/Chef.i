Izakaya

A bar with cocktails, beer, sake, yakitori, ramen and flavorful bites that capture the essence of Japan’s beloved drinking culture. No table service, order by QR code, limited booths available for reservations, WALK-INS WELCOME!!!!

temaki-ya

A hand roll bar that offers limited small plates, a wide selection of sake, Japanese whisky and cocktails. Reservations preferred.

What is Izakaya?

The Japanese word izakaya (居酒屋) is made up of three kanji with the meaning, in order, “stay-drink-place.” A spot to grab a drink, settle in, and get comfortable
