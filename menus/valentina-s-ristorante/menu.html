Are you looking for authentic Italian American cuisine?

Then visit one of the best Italian restaurants in Westchester County, NY!


Here at Valentina's in the Fleetwood section of Mount Vernon, NY, we take pride in offering our guests a delicious Italian American restaurant experience.


We offer a wide array of pasta dishes, seafood dishes, chicken & meat dishes, along with delicious appetizers & desserts that will make your mouth water.


Also, if you would like to enjoy delicious, fresh Italian cuisine at your family event, graduation party, office party, or religious celebration, call us about our catering services today.


We can gladly host your , Family Party, Graduation Party , & Holiday Parties at our restaurant in our Spacious, Second-Floor party room/party space. The Convenient Fleetwood Parking Garage (ProPark) is located just a short, half block away on 42 W. Broad Street.
For the true Italian American experience, stop by one of the most authentic Italian restaurants near Yonkers, NY. We are conveniently located in the  Fleetwood section  in Mount Vernon, NY.


Our restaurant is located on the border of Yonkers, Bronxville, and Pelham.
We provide catering to residents and businesses throughout Bronxville, Yonkers, Mount Vernon, Pelham, and many surrounding areas throughout Westchester County.
