Bin 100 Restaurant would be happy to be your party venue of choice for your next special event. We can help you host the perfect party at our Italian restaurant, featuring our incredible catering menu. If you and your guests would love to dine on delicious chicken, seafood, or pasta dishes, we have the perfect menu options for you. Find out more about our party venue in Milford, CT.


Planning any type of party or event can be stressful, especially when it comes to deciding the right menu. At Bin 100 Restaurant, we feature a wide range of catering options to best fit you and your party's needs. From delicious appetizers and salads to fresh pastas and classic entrees, our chefs will take care of you. Contact us today to learn more about our options or to order yours today! We are proud to deliver across Milford, Stratford, Fairfield, Bridgeport, CT, and the surrounding areas.
