Customer Stories

aleshia hil

The decor is beautiful ! I had the street corn which is very good I had the lobster tacos which were really really good . The taco bowl with the shrimp and chicken is really good my bf said the chicken was seasoned very well. The frozen strawberry margarita was really good as well . You will really enjoy it . The only thing that got me was the server wasnt familiar with the drink menu and in the serving industry it is vital to know the menu items being that it was my first time at the restaurant she was very good other than that I went at around 11:05 pm so it was not really packed

Emily Anna Hanson

Visited during the Cherry Blossom festival. Have to say, I am living for the branding and vibe of this place. Our drinks were delicious and on theme. We tried out the nachos, and wholly smokes, I would give an arm and a leg to have them again. One day, when I visit DC again, I truly hope Pink Taco is still there.

Godden Garden

Went for a dinner party. The table was set up before we got there. Everything was organized and professional. The burrito was cold in the middle. The rest of the food was good. The drinks were good too. Its ok. Might visit again

Rosie H

Went here for an early dinner on Saturday. We were seated in the bar area. The chips and salsa are completely addicting! I got the chicken quesadilla and it might just be the best chicken quesadilla I have ever had in my life. Packed with amazing flavor. Our server was attentive and friendly. And the music playing was fun old school top 40s.

Kayleigh Crandell

Beautiful taco spot! Expensive but so worth it. Jacia was an awesome waitress and the brunch DJ was so much fun. We ordered the chicken drumettes starter, a margarita flight, a michelada, fried lobster tacos, and the OG pink tacos. Everything was delicious, I'd definitely come back to try more and would recommend to anyone in the area! I bet parties here would be super fun.

Mary Carmona

Went in last night. Wasn't too busy. Was greeted by the few people at the host desk and seated right away. Server came within minutes put in our order and got our food within 15 minutes. I ordered the lobster enchiladas and it did not disappoint. Want to go back for happy hour.

Tabitha Reem
