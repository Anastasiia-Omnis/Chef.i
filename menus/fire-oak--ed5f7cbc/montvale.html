    Fire & Oak Montvale has proudly been a trusted destination for discerning guests since 2009. Situated along the bustling Chestnut Ridge Road in Montvale, New Jersey, our expansive dining areas are designed to captivate your senses. With an impressive selection of sushi, a delectable raw bar, prime steaks, and signature seafood dishes, Fire & Oak ensures that there is something to satisfy every palate. Whether it's an intimate date night or a professional business meal, our restaurant offers an inviting atmosphere and top-notch service to enhance your dining experience.
