At The Oak House, we take pride in offering a drinking experience like no other. With our handcrafted cocktails, 12 beers on tap, and a carefully curated selection of wines by the glass, we guarantee you'll find your perfect drink. Whether you're a cocktail connoisseur or a beer lover, our bar has something for everyone. And the best part? The Oak House vibe is always lively and fun, making every sip even more enjoyable. Don't miss out on our Happy Hour, available Monday through Friday from 4:00-6:00 in our bar and lounge. Join us for a delightful evening of great drinks and good times.
