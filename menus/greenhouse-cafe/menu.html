    Welcome to Greenhouse Cafe!

    Since 1979, Greenhouse Cafe has been a staple in the Bay Ridge community. We have a full size bar and three dining rooms, which may also be used for a variety of catered affairs. 

    We have tons of delicious dishes to choose from and a beautiful decor, we assure you that you will leave feeling satisfied and craving to come back for more. See below for our menus and we are looking forward to serving you!
