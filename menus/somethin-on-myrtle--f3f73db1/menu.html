Unique, Flavorful, and Always Fresh

Frequently Asked Questions

Have questions about our business? We've provided answers below.

  • Why is Somethin' On Myrtle so popular?

    Somethin' On Myrtle has gained immense popularity for its unique blend of Asian and Caribbean flavors, making it a go-to destination for foodies seeking a one-of-a-kind dining experience. Our Asian bistro stands out from the crowd by expertly infusing traditional Asian culinary traditions and tastes with the vibrant spices and ingredients of the Caribbean, resulting in an innovative and mouthwatering menu. 


    The diverse and tantalizing offerings at Somethin' On Myrtle showcase the culinary prowess and creativity that sets our Asian bistro apart. Our inviting atmosphere, superior service, and commitment to fostering a socially diverse and inclusive environment further contribute to our popularity. Somethin' On Myrtle is a beloved and sought-after dining destination for those craving an unforgettable fusion of flavors at an exceptional Asian bistro.

  • Where is your restaurant located?

    Somethin' On Myrtle is conveniently located at (and named after) 503 Myrtle Ave. in Brooklyn, New York.

  • How do I get in touch?

    To inquire about reservations and restaurant hours, or if you have any questions about our menu, you may reach us directly by phone at (718) 789-1111. You may also contact us by using the convenient forms found on this site.

  • What are your business hours?

    Somethin' On Myrtle's current business hours are as follows:


    Monday to Thursday: 2:00 PM - 11:00 PM (Happy Hour: 4:00 PM - 7:00 PM)

    Friday & Saturday: 2:00 PM - 1:00 AM

    Sunday: 12:00 PM - 11:00 PM (Brunch: 12:00 PM - 4:00 PM) (Dinner: 5:00 PM - 10:00 PM)


    Check back soon for updates on our brunch menu and hours. Current hours are subject to change.
