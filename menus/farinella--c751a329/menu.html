A New York
Classic Pizza.

Since day one, Farinella has been a "rare find" in NYC—a place where Roman traditions and New York grit come together to create something unforgettable. Located at 1792 Broadway, we’ve become a neighborhood staple, serving fresh, bold flavors with the kind of charm that keeps New Yorkers coming back.


From our classic Margherita to the indulgent Tartufo Nero, and even a bite of our legendary bomboloni, everything we make is baked fresh and served with attitude. This isn’t just pizza; it’s a piece of NYC history.
