$49 Pre-Theater Menu

Enjoy our $49 Per Person Pre-Theater Menu before heading off to see your Broadway or Off-Broadway show!

Available daily until 6:30 PM.

Includes 1 Welcome Drink
Classic Mojito, Classic Margarita, Red Sangria, House Wine, Well Drinks, or Beer

1 Appetizer
Empanada Sampler, Chips & Guac, Crispy Fried Pork, Cuban Fried Chicken, or Avocado Salad

1 Entrée
Served with 2 sides (white or yellow rice, black beans, or side salad).
"*" Denotes items served with 1 side only.
Braised Beef, Grilled Skirt Steak, Slow Roasted Pork, Mango Glazed Salmon, Garlic Chicken, or *Stuffed Peppers(v)

1 Dessert
Flan(v) or Churros(v)

You can also add on a Cuban Coffee to complete your meal!
Cafe Con Leche, Single Espresso, Double Espresso, Cappuccino, and Fresh Brew available.


Please note that the price does not include tax, gratuity, or additional beverages.
Dine-in only. Order must be placed before 6:30 PM.
