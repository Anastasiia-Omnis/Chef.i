    • Welcome to Ombra!

    Ombra is a Venetian-inspired Salumeria & Bar created by Owner & Certified Sommelier, Gabi Lombardi.

    Our mission is to provide first class hospitality while celebrating Italy's vibrant culinary culture.

    About the menus

    Our menu offers a modern twist on the traditional Venetian "bacaro", or wine bar. We offer a variety of imported meats and cheeses alongside "cicchetti" & seasonal small plates. Our bar highlights the best wines from Northern Italy and a variety of classic & signature craft cocktails.
