Bienvenue chez Risbo Restaurant

French Rotisserie Restaurant + Bar in Brooklyn

Risbo is your neighborhood spot for healthy comfort food, natural wines, and good vibes. We are a Michelin-Recommended fast-casual rotisserie with a full bar and a beautiful garden a true plant oasis. We're an inviting restaurant stationed in the heart of Prospect Lefferts Garden, Brooklyn, and the place you come to dine when your belly and soul need nourishment. We are open for Lunch, Dinner and Brunch!

Risbo Specialties in Brooklyn

Dinner, Lunch & Brunch With a French Flare! 

Welcome to Risbo Restaurant, where we put a Brooklyn twist on classic French rotisserie dishes! Our proteins are marinated and dry-brined for two days, locking in unbeatable moisture and flavor with every bite.                                                       

For lunch, we serve up French-inspired favorites, all given our own signature touch. For dinner, indulge in our legendary roasted chicken, pork, duck, leg of lamb, and whole branzino, accompanied by a selection of plant-based sides to mix and match your meal. For Brunch, try our delicious French specialty as the Oeuf Brouill!

We look forward to welcoming you, in the heart of Prospect Lefferts Garden.
