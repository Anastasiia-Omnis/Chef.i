    • Located in the heart of Downtown Manhattan, Bowery Meat Company is a modern steakhouse by Mercer Street Hospitality’s John McDonald. Chef David DiSalvo offers the finest 30- day dry-aged Prime steaks, along with indulgent American Wagyu and A5 Japanese Wagyu in a sophisticated, friendly setting. Pair your meal with a crafted cocktail or a fine wine by the glass or bottle, or end the evening with a soothing digestif. Join us for Sunday Supper, a convivial four-course prix-fixe meal to end your weekend on a high note.

    New York Times gives BMC a 2-star "critics pick" review. Peter Wells recommends: Broiled oysters; duck lasagna; lamb chops; veal chop; cheeseburger; côte de boeuf

    More Reviews: New York Observer, Tasting Table, New York Magazine and New York Post Table-Hopping.
