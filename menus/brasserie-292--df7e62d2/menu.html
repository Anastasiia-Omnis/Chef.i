    • Welcome to Brasserie 292

    Brasserie 292 combines the old-world charm of Poughkeepsie's historic Main Street district with a timeless, classic Brasserie fare. Diners can choose from a menu that boasts an impressive range of selections, from our Raw Bar to the Plats Du Jour, and includes a diverse wine, spirits and beer offering. Once a popular garment district, our inviting dining room has been transformed into a spacious haven. We have a simple philosophy at Brasserie 292: use fine local ingredients whenever possible, coupled with traditional cookery to create an incomparable and exceptional dining experience.
