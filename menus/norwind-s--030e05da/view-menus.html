    Classic Cocktails

    • Mojito

      1 teaspoon of sugar syrup, 6 fresh mint leaves, lime juice, 1 ounces of rum, club soda, a dash of bitters, mango, coconut, passion fruit, strawberry, watermelon

    • Old Fashioned

      1 cube, a dash of bitters, 1 teaspoon of water, 1 ounces of bourbon or rye whiskey (lemon peel twist garnish/orange)

    • Negroni

      1 ounce of gin, Campari, sweet vermouth, orange bitters (orange peel garnish)

    • Manhattan

      A dash of bitters, 2 ounces of rye whiskey, ounce of sweet vermouth (cherry garnish)

    • Espresso Martini

      1 to 2 ounces of vodka, 1 ounce of coffee liqueur, 1 ounce of espresso or cold brew concentrate, ounce of simple syrup, 3 espresso beans garnish ice

    • Lychee Martini

      6 ounces of vodka, 4 ounces of lychee juice or syrup, 1 ounce of vermouth, 2 lychee garnish

    • Cosmopolitan

      1 ounces of vodka, ounces of triple sec, ounces of lime juice, ounce of cranberry juice (orange peel strip garnish)

    • Long Island Iced Tea

      ounces of vodka, ounces of gin, ounces of white tequila, ounces of white rum, ounce of crme de menthe, 1 ounces of lemon juice, 1 teaspoon of fine sugar, cola, cracked ice (lime garnish)
