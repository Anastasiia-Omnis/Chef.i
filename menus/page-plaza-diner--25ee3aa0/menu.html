The Page Plaza Diner located at 75 Page Avenue in the Tottenville area of Staten Island, New York has been a favorite to many on the South Shore since it’s Grand opening in 2007. Page Plaza Diner is nestled close to the Outerbridge, has been one of Tottenville’s best kept secrets.

Enjoy a warm and cozy, yet modern atmosphere, when you’re greeted at the door, and seated by one of the owners. You feel the comfort as soon as you walk in.

Delivery

If you can’t get to the Diner we can deliver. Page Plaza Diner is open 7 days a week/24 hours a day. You can also order take-out 24/7.

Catering

Page Plaza Diner offers on and off premise catering. We have an elaborate catering room that seats up to 100. You can have a sit down dinner or a buffet style on premises. We can do off premise catering for as many as you like. See our Menu page for our catering choices.

Favorites

People always ask us what to order. Everything we serve is great. Some of the favorites here are:

  • Chicken Kebab Platter
  • Sourdough Steak Melt
  • Grilled Mixed Seafood over Mesclun Salad
