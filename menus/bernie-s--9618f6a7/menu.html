    • Welome to Bernie's!

    We are open every night, Monday - Sunday. We do not accept dinner reservations and the restaurant and bar are filled on a first-come, first-served basis. Once the restaurant is full, the list for the evening begins, and our experienced hosts will provide you with an estimate for a table. Our largest booth fits 8, maybe 9, but it's a squeeze! Saturday & Sunday, prepare to show up well before you’d like to eat dinner — there is a line before we open at 4 pm! While you wait, we invite you to enjoy a drink at the bar or on the sidewalk during warmer months — a quintessential part of the Bernie’s experience. For shorter or no wait times, consider visiting earlier in the week, and during non-peak hours.

    For any further questions please send us an email.
