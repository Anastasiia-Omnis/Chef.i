    Aperitivo Hour

    From a refreshing spritz to our signature Oggi Martini, pull up a seat at the bar for Aperitivo Hour. Join us starting at 4 PM every day, just steps from Central Park and the Beacon Theater.

    Pair your cocktail with market-driven small plates that showcase the best ingredients of the season. Whether you’re meeting friends, dining before a show, or simply looking for an elevated spot on the Upper West Side, Aperitivo Hour is the perfect start to your evening.

    Reserve Now

    Vinyl Night

    Every Thursday evening, the bar at Sempre Oggi comes alive with analog sound. Beginning at 8 PM, our friend David Kastin shares selections from his personal vinyl collection. It's an eclectic mix designed to set the perfect mood for and evening on the Upper West Side.

    Sip on a spritz or a glass of Italian wine while you take in the music and the atmosphere of the room.

    Reserve Now

    Brunch

    Our brunch is quintessentially UWS with an Italian twist. Join us every Saturday and Sunday from 11am–3pm for a menu that blends tradition with seasonality. Dishes showcase our in-house pastas and fresh-baked focaccia, paired with the day’s best ingredients from New York City farms and markets.
