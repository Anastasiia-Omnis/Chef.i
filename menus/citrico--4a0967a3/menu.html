Welcome

Come check out Citrico, Brooklyns purveyor of fresh authentic Mexican food. Located in the illustrious Prospect Heights neighborhood of Brooklyn, New York, Citrico opened in the spring of 2014 and is known for serving dishes with bright, bold tastes and recipes that pair authentic Mexican ingredients with citric flavors. Regulars love it and newcomers cant wait to return. Have your next meal here and savor the food that El Sabor Citrico de Mexico has to offer!
