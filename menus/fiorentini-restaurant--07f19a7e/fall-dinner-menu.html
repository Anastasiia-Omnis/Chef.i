    Seasonal, Sustainable Italian Dining in New Jersey

    Fiorentini exclusively works with local farmers using the most sustainable practices focusing on the health of their soils. Healthy soils culture an ecosystem for animals and vegetables to thrive without the need for antibiotics, hormones, herbicides, or pesticides. In the Winter season, they are fed non-GMO grains, organic vegetables, and/or hay mix which helps create the healthiest soil conditions for vegetables to grow till harvest without the use of treatments. 

    Chef Antonio curates a new menu each season.
