Chiddy’s Cheesesteak Restaurants

Philadelphia is famous for the Liberty Bell, Rocky, the 76ers, and Philly cheesesteaks. This meaty, hot, cheesy sandwich is unforgettable and for decades was worth the long drive to sample one on its home turf. But not anymore. That great sammich you long for is now available locally on Long Island and Queens, but you’ll find the best only at Chiddy’s CheeseSteaks.

When you’re craving this regional treat there’s no need to travel down I-95, or to Google “philly cheese steak restaurants near me”, “cheesesteak place near me”, or “cheese steak restaurants near me.” For real-deal, authentic Philly cheesesteaks on Long Island, and so much more, visit one of our four locations in Farmingdale, Commack, West Islip, and Sunnyside Queens for specials you won’t want to miss! ‘Stop by our Food Truck in Bay Shore while you’re out shopping, or make our truck or our cheesesteak restaurants your dining destination!

Chiddy’s Cheese Steaks are standouts, but we offer something for everyone! When you’re hungry for a meal or just a nosh, be sure to try our delectable beef and cheddar empanadas, creamy mac n’ cheese, spicy nachos, and crunchy mozzarella sticks. We have a kid's menu, offer lighter fare, hot dogs, our spin on Mexican favorites, homemade desserts, and so much more! Check out our promo specials and don’t forget to sign up for a Chiddy’s Card. (Order 10 sandwiches and the 11th is free!)
