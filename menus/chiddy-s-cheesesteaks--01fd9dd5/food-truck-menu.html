The Fry Bar

People have asked us why we take the time to fresh-cut our fries. And then they try the fries. And then they realize how much better a fresh, crispy, hand-cut fry really is than any other french fry. The aroma alone is enough to see why all of the extra effort is worth it. We could go on for hours about how awesome these fries are, and how when you have one you just cant help but go in and finish the whole plate of them, but instead, you should just try them and see for yourself

  • Fresh Cut Fries

    This one speaks for itself. The fries are fresh cut, crispy, and golden brown. Having just one is not an option

  • Cheezy Fries

    Our fresh cut fries topped with melted, gooey cheese sauce

Choose From Our Gourmet Fries!

  • Cheesesteak Fries

    A combination of our ribeye steak, hand cut fries, and melted cheese whiz. An instant classic

  • Disco Fries

    Remember those fries you used to get at the diner? Topped with steak, melted mozzarella cheese and smothered in gravy? Well, look no further! An old time classic resurrected and served hot and fresh

  • Hell Fries

    A name that does all the talking for us. Juicy chicken, melted provolone cheese, smothered in hot sauce on top of our crispy fries. Offered with a side of bleu cheese or ranch to cool things down

  • Squealin Fries

    Crispy bacon on top of hand cut fries with melted cheese. A savory combination that will never let you down

  • Irish Nachos

    Traditionally made with potato chips, our Irish nachos are turned up a notch my using our fresh cut fries instead. Topped with award winning chili, homemade chipotle aioli, sour cream, jalapeos, tomatoes and diced raw onions

  • Huevos n Potatoes

    Fresh cut fries, topped with ribeye steak, eggs over easy, melted cheese whiz and homemade chipotle aioli. An original from our days as just a food truck, and a customer favorite ever since

Other Sides

Not feeling the fresh cut fries today? We have you covered. Our crispy, crunchy onion rings, homemade mac n cheese, gooey, stringy mozzarella sticks or divine sweet potato fries pair excellently with any of our sandwiches

  • Homemade Mac n Cheese

    How can you go wrong with mac n cheese? Chiddys homemade recipe kicks it up a notch over the rest

  • Homemade Chili

    Deemed #1 on Long Island by Newsday, our chili is savory with just a little kick at the end. Made with seasoned ground beef, small chunks of tomato, onions, peppers and kidney beans. Ask for it with a side of sour cream or shredded cheddar to finish it off right

  • Onion Rings

    Our gourmet onion rings are golden brown and crispy. Delicious all on their own, even better when dipped into any of our homemade aioli sauces

  • Sweet Potato Fries

    A salty-sweet combo that pairs with any sandwich on the menu, the sweet potato fries stormed onto the scene in 2020 and win the hearts of our fans every day

  • Side Salad

    Plain and simple, fresh ingredients make a difference. This side salad proves that. Made with hand chopped lettuce and tomato and your choice of other hand chopped veggies, this salad compliments any meal

  • Mozzarella Sticks

    Who doesnt love mozzarella sticks? Stringy, cheesy dunked in homemade marinara sauce. Crunchy on the outside and melty on the inside

  • Homemade Chips & Cheese

    Hot, house-made tortilla chips with sides of melted cheese. The crunch from biting into these salty snacks speaks volumes about how fresh and delicious these are

Cheesesteak Fries & Other Sides

Chiddy’s is not only home to the best cheesesteaks outside (and probably inside) Philadelphia, but our cheesesteak fries do not take a back seat either. Try ‘em, and you’ll be as excited to return for our fry bar and other sides as you are for our legendary cheesesteaks! Feast your eyes on our menu - what’ll it be today? The Mac and Cheese with all the ooey, gooey warmth of a hug and that indescribable something, something else that only Chiddy’s has to offer! And you know you’ve been dreaming about our delectable sweet potato fries ever since your last visit! With just the right touch of seasoning, our crunchy on the outside, and creamy on the inside fries are hard to beat! Salad lovers, we admire your restraint. And have not forgotten you. Chiddy’s salads are a buffet unto themselves, with each ingredient chopped or diced to perfection and for your total enjoyment!

Sweet Treats

Chiddy’s is much more than a place to stop by when you’re in the mood for a treat. We’re a dining destination. We have savory courses, and salads to round out your meal, you didn’t think we’d leave out desert, right? Chiddy’s makes the best comfort food after all. We combined our empanada-making skills with some dessert favorites like apple pie, peanut butter cups, and bananas and Nutella. Our dessert empanadas are melty and indulgent, and the perfect way to cap off a meal from Chiddy’s, or just for a late night snack. One of our top sellers is our apple pie empanada! Or, if dessert empanadas aren’t up your alley, we also have a brownie on the menu that is moist and mouthwatering. So whenever you are in the neighborhood, or not, it is always worth a detour to visit Chiddy’s CheeseSteaks for our expansive range of delectable offerings

Get Delicious Dessert Empanadas at Chiddy’s

Whatever you choose, you cannot go wrong! And if you are like us, and want to reward yourself after a hard day, bring the family on over for Chiddy’s dessert empanadas and more! Whatever your pleasure, a chocolaty brownie, apple pie empanada, banana Nutella empanada, or the savory and sweet peanut butter cup empanada, you’ll never go home empty or disappointed! Plan to visit Chiddy’s today!
