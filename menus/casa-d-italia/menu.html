An online ordering system enables food businesses such as table service restaurants, coffee shops, or food trucks to accept orders online for pickup, delivery, and dine-in.

Online ordering unlocks an additional revenue stream that empowers food businesses to stay competitive and attract new and recurring customers.

Can I use GloriaFood for selling food on social media?

Yes, you can copy the smart ordering link we provide in your restaurant dashboard and paste it to:

  • Your Google Business Profile
  • Your Yelp / TripAdvisor profiles
  • Facebook’s Start Order button
  • Your Instagram and TikTok bios
  • And any other social media platform

The link will automatically redirect customers to your online menu, so they don’t have to go looking for it elsewhere.

Can I use GloriaFood for contactless table ordering?

Yes, GloriaFood offers an on premise ordering feature that you can enable to generate individual QR codes for your tables.

That way, guests can scan the QR code to browse the menu, place an order, and pay for it, all from their smartphones.

Can I use GloriaFood for room service orders in my hotel?

Yes, GloriaFood offers a hotel ordering system that can be used for room service, as well as other areas of the hotel such as sunbeds, conference rooms and banqueting areas, spa, lounge, etc.

Guests can charge orders to their rooms and pay everything when they check out for a smooth, cashless experience.
