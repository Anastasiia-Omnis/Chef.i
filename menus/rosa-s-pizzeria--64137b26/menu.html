A FAMILY TRADITION SINCE 1975

IT’S ALL GOOD!

Welcome to the newest Rosa’s Pizzeria. We are now open in Williamsburg serving up all your favorites from the last 40 years. For the first time ever you can order delivery from Rosas, and right here on our website ! In 1975, our father and his seven brothers opened up the first and original Rosa’s Pizzeria in Maspeth and have been crafting the famous upside down ever since, winning over pizza lovers throughout New York City and Queens. We are so excited to share that square and many other incredible, handcrafted slices and a menu with full Italian fare, here in Brooklyn. We invite you to drop in and make our family, your family.
