    Gage & Tollner Celebration Cakes

    Specialty cakes are available for your next celebratory evening with at least 72 hours notice. Cakes must be enjoyed onsite. 

    All cakes are three layers, with no sprinkles or food coloring. Special requests and complex customizations for celebration cakes may incur an additional fee and require more notice for Chef Kathryn. 

    6” (serves up to 8) - $95
    8” (serves 12-16) - $145
    10” (serves up to 20) - $190


    Place Cake Order

    • G&T Birthday Cake

      Choice of light vanilla cake or rich chocolate sourcream cake with a choice of chocolate or vanilla Swiss meringue buttercream. Decorated with chocolate cookie crumble and fresh flowers. Nut-free.

    • Signature Coconut Cake

      A large format version of our signature menu item. Fluffy coconut lime cake layered with coconut pastry cream and fresh lime curd. Finished with coconut Swiss meringue buttercream, cashew pink peppercorn brittle, fresh flowers and toasted coconut. Can be made nut-free.

    • Lemon Poppy Olive Oil

      Supple lemon poppy olive oil cake layers, filled with fresh lemon curd, and finished with Swiss meringue buttercream. Decorated with candied lemon zest and fresh flowers. Nut free.

    • Carrot Cake

      Walnut carrot cake with coconut and a classic cream cheese frosting. No raisins! Decorated with walnuts and toasted coconut.

    • Brooklyn Blackout Cake

      Dark chocolate pudding layered dark chocolate cake with dark chocolate ganache, finished with coffee amaro sauce. Nut free.
