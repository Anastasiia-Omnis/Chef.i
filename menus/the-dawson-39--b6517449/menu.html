About

Discover Dawson 39 Irish Bar & Restaurant — the newest gem in the heart of Midtown NYC, just steps from Bryant Park and off 6th Avenue. We're a family-owned Irish bar and restaurant proudly serving classic dishes, Irish-American favorites, signature burgers, along a curated selection of craft beers and signature cocktails. Our elegant, spacious, bi-level venue features a large bar, HD TV’s throughout and comfortable seating; an ideal venue for private and semi-private events.

Join us at Dawson 39 Restaurant & Sports Bar for lunch, dinner, a delicious bar menu. Whether it’s a quick bite, a round of after-work drinks or a special celebration, stop by and discover why Dawson 39 is the neighborhood’s new go-to spot. Explore our party packages and book your next event today!
