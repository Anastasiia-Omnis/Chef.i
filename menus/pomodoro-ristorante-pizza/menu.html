About Us

Get to know Us

We are a family-owned business located conveniently across from Morristown train station. With over twenty years of experience, Pomodoro has turned into your best place to find some of the most exquisite and delicious foods available. We specialize in Italian Cuisine that’s made fresh daily using the best ingredients guaranteed! We make our dough daily and use cheese made 100% from milk, quality meats, and fresh produce to top our well-known and exceptionally tasty Pizza pies.
