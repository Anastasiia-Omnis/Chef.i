Our Story

Are you in the mood for delicious food using fresh ingredients at an affordable price? If so, then we are the destination for you. Lee Xing Restaurant offers authentic and delicious tasting Chinese cuisine in Bronx, NY. Lee Xing's convenient location and affordable prices make our restaurant a natural choice for dine-in or take-out meals in the Bronx community. Our restaurant is known for its variety in taste and high quality fresh ingredients. Come and experience our friendly atmosphere and excellent service.
