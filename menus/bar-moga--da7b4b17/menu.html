Welcome to Bar Moga

Bar Moga is a 1920s-era Japanese cocktail bar with jazz music, specializing in Craft Cocktails , Shochu, Japanese whiskey-based libations, and Yoshoku comfort food. Embodying the best this era of Japan had to offer.

Food

Yōshoku (洋食), or "western" dishes, are comfort food in Japan that boomed during the Taisho period, lending itself perfectly to the Bar Moga concept.

Private Events

Our private dining room offers an intimate and exclusive setting for up to 20 guests. It is the perfect choice if you are looking to host a smaller gathering in a more secluded atmosphere. Your guests will enjoy a dedicated space with personalized service and a unique ambiance.

Our bar is also available for a full buy-out.  Different packages are available depending on date and time.
