WELCOME TO RELISH WINE BAR & GRILL! 

Established in 2019, Relish is a cozy restaurant, bar, and grill located in Ansonia, CT. We make fine dining approachable, as our menu focuses on taking classics that you love and modernizing them with fresh, inspired ingredients.
