Our Story

Long Hing Restaurant offers authentic and delicious tasting Chinese cuisine in Montclair, NJ. Long Hing's convenient location and affordable prices make our restaurant a natural choice for eat-in or take-out meals in the Montclair community. Our restaurant is known for its variety in taste and high quality fresh ingredients. Come and experience our friendly atmosphere and excellent service.
